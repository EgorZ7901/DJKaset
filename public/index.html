DJ KASET

Wedding & Event DJ

DJ KASET always knew she wanted to play music. After graduating from University of British Columbia, she pursued DJing as a hobby, which eventually became a second career. Having 15 years of competitive Ballroom Dancing experience, she knows how to get people to the dance floor.

From DJ Kaset: "Music is therapy for heart and soul. It opens people, brings out memories, and can improve any event. There is no better feeling than having a full dance floor of happy people."

She has played a number of events nationally and internationally, including weddings, graduations, birthday and anniversary parties, store openings, film events and more. She is familiar with venues like Hotel Georgia, Hotel Vancouver, Terminal City Club, Brix & Mortar, Science World, Hycroft Manor, Hotel Fairmont in Whistler, Nita Lake Lodge, Four Seasons Hotel, Squamish Lil'wat Cultural Centre, Sheraton Hotel, Bridges, Brock house restaurant, the Loft at Earls, Pinnacle Hotel and more.

FAQ

Do you meet with clients?

Yes, of course! Every client and event is different. I offer a coffee meet or a skype/telephone call to see if I will be a good fit for your event. However, a meeting is not mandatory and everything can be arranged over email as well.

Do you travel for weddings?

Yes, absolutely! However, there is an additional cost for anything outside of Greater Vancouver area. Please inquire for a quote.

How far do you travel?

I often travel as far as Whistler and performed at popular event venues including Nita Lake Lodge, Fairmont Whistler and Squamish Lil'wat Cultural Centre.

What equipment do you provide?

2 x Yamaha DXR-8 (800 Watt self-powered speakers) 2 x Speaker stands 1 x MacBook Pro 1 x Professional handheld wireless microphone 1 x Disco Light. Additional equipment can be rented as per event's needs.

What music will you play at our event?

I send all of my clients a music request sheet where you can be as detailed as you'd like about style of the music or specific songs you'd like me to play at your event. You can also specify what songs or style you do not want to hear at your event.

When do you arrive to set up?

I arrive 1-2 hours before an event depending on the size and complexity of the set up. Additional arrangements can be made as per request.
